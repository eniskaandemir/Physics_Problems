# Task 01 – Series and Parallel Circuit

## Problem Statement

Three resistors are given:

$$ R_1 = 15\,\Omega $$

$$ R_2 = 30\,\Omega $$

$$ R_3 = 50\,\Omega $$

The battery voltage is

$$ V = 12\,\text{V} $$

Determine:

1. the equivalent resistance when connected in series
2. the battery current in the series case
3. the equivalent resistance when connected in parallel
4. the battery current in the parallel case

## Theory

### Series Connection

For resistors in series:

$$ R_{\text{eq}} = R_1 + R_2 + R_3 $$

### Parallel Connection

For resistors in parallel:

$$ \frac{1}{R_{\text{eq}}} = \frac{1}{R_1} + \frac{1}{R_2} + \frac{1}{R_3} $$

### Ohm's Law

$$ I = \frac{V}{R} $$

## Step-by-Step Solution

### 1. Series Equivalent Resistance

$$ R_{\text{series}} = 15 + 30 + 50 $$

$$ R_{\text{series}} = 95\,\Omega $$

### 2. Series Current

$$ I_{\text{series}} = \frac{12}{95} $$

$$ I_{\text{series}} \approx 0.126\,\text{A} $$

### 3. Parallel Equivalent Resistance

$$ \frac{1}{R_{\text{parallel}}} = \frac{1}{15} + \frac{1}{30} + \frac{1}{50} $$

Convert fractions into decimals:

$$ \frac{1}{15} = 0.0667 $$

$$ \frac{1}{30} = 0.0333 $$

$$ \frac{1}{50} = 0.0200 $$

Add them:

$$ \frac{1}{R_{\text{parallel}}} = 0.0667 + 0.0333 + 0.0200 $$

$$ \frac{1}{R_{\text{parallel}}} = 0.1200 $$

Therefore:

$$ R_{\text{parallel}} = \frac{1}{0.1200} $$

$$ R_{\text{parallel}} \approx 8.33\,\Omega $$

### 4. Parallel Current

$$ I_{\text{parallel}} = \frac{12}{8.33} $$

$$ I_{\text{parallel}} \approx 1.44\,\text{A} $$

## Final Result

### Series Circuit

$$ R_{\text{series}} = 95\,\Omega $$

$$ I_{\text{series}} \approx 0.126\,\text{A} $$

### Parallel Circuit

$$ R_{\text{parallel}} \approx 8.33\,\Omega $$

$$ I_{\text{parallel}} \approx 1.44\,\text{A} $$

## Interpretation

Series connections increase total resistance because resistors are added directly.

Parallel connections decrease total resistance because current can flow through multiple branches simultaneously.
