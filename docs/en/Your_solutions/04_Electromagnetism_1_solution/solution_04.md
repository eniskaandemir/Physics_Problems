# Task 04 – Force Comparison

## Problem Statement

Determine the electric force and the gravitational force between an electron and a proton in a hydrogen atom.

Use:

$$
r = 5.3 \times 10^{-11} \text{ m}
$$

Also determine the ratio

$$
\frac{F_e}{F_g}
$$

## Theory

The electric force magnitude is

$$
F_e = k \frac{e^2}{r^2}
$$

The gravitational force magnitude is

$$
F_g = G \frac{m_e m_p}{r^2}
$$

Constants:

$$
k = 8.99 \times 10^9
$$

$$
G = 6.67 \times 10^{-11}
$$

$$
e = 1.60 \times 10^{-19} \text{ C}
$$

$$
m_e = 9.11 \times 10^{-31} \text{ kg}
$$

$$
m_p = 1.67 \times 10^{-27} \text{ kg}
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

### 1. Electric Force

Use

$$
F_e = k \frac{e^2}{r^2}
$$

Compute the square of the charge:

$$
e^2 = (1.60 \times 10^{-19})^2 = 2.56 \times 10^{-38}
$$

Compute the square of the distance:

$$
r^2 = (5.3 \times 10^{-11})^2 = 2.809 \times 10^{-21}
$$

Now substitute:

$$
F_e = 8.99 \times 10^9 \cdot \frac{2.56 \times 10^{-38}}{2.809 \times 10^{-21}}
$$

$$
F_e \approx 8.2 \times 10^{-8} \text{ N}
$$

### 2. Gravitational Force

Use

$$
F_g = G \frac{m_e m_p}{r^2}
$$

First compute the product of masses:

$$
m_e m_p = (9.11 \times 10^{-31})(1.67 \times 10^{-27})
$$

$$
m_e m_p \approx 1.52 \times 10^{-57}
$$

Substitute:

$$
F_g = 6.67 \times 10^{-11} \cdot \frac{1.52 \times 10^{-57}}{2.809 \times 10^{-21}}
$$

$$
F_g \approx 3.6 \times 10^{-47} \text{ N}
$$

### 3. Ratio

$$
\frac{F_e}{F_g} = \frac{8.2 \times 10^{-8}}{3.6 \times 10^{-47}}
$$

$$
\frac{F_e}{F_g} \approx 2.3 \times 10^{39}
$$

## Final Result

Electric force:

$$
F_e \approx 8.2 \times 10^{-8} \text{ N}
$$

Gravitational force:

$$
F_g \approx 3.6 \times 10^{-47} \text{ N}
$$

Ratio:

$$
\frac{F_e}{F_g} \approx 2.3 \times 10^{39}
$$

## Interpretation

The electric attraction between the proton and electron is enormously stronger than their gravitational attraction. At atomic scales, gravity is completely negligible compared to the electric interaction.
