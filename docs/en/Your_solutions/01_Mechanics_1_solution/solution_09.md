# Task 09 – Momentum Comparison

## Problem Statement

Compare momentum of:

- a $2$ g fly at $10$ m/s
- a $60$ g tennis ball at $1$ m/s

---

## Theory

Momentum is:

$$
p = mv
$$

Mass must be in kilograms.

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

### 1. Convert Units

$$
m_{\text{fly}} = 0.002 \text{ kg}
$$

$$
m_{\text{ball}} = 0.06 \text{ kg}
$$

---

### 2. Compute Momentum

Fly:

$$
p = 0.002 \cdot 10 = 0.02
$$

Ball:

$$
p = 0.06 \cdot 1 = 0.06
$$

---

## Final Result

$$
p_{\text{ball}} > p_{\text{fly}}
$$

---

## Interpretation

Even though the fly moves faster, the tennis ball has greater momentum due to its much larger mass.
